# Katalume brand mark

**Katalume** is pronounced “kat-uh-LOOM.” The name combines **kata**, deliberate
practice that forges mastery, with **lume**, light or illumination—the moment a
hard problem clicks.

## Ensemble Spark

The Katalume mark depicts three practice paths converging into one illuminated
result. It connects the rigor of repeated kata with the moment of insight at the
heart of lume, while retaining a subtle visual reference to ensemble learning.

The geometry was drawn specifically for Katalume and is not based on a stock
icon, template, letterform, or generated logo pack.

## Primary asset

- `public/brand/katalume-mark.svg` — canonical full-color vector mark.
- `src/app/components/BrandMark.tsx` — product implementation of the same
  geometry, including its responsive sizing and subtle UI shadow.

## Color

The mark uses the Blue Eclipse product palette:

- Eclipse: `#0F0E47`
- Midnight: `#272757`
- Core indigo: `#505081`
- Signal lavender: `#8686AC`
- White: `#FFFFFF`

## Usage

- Keep at least one input-node diameter of clear space around the tile.
- Use the full-color tile on light, dark, or photographic backgrounds.
- Do not rotate, recolor individual paths, stretch, or add text inside the tile.
- Use the `Katalume` wordmark beside the mark in Plus Jakarta Sans, weight 700.
- The minimum recommended digital size is 24 × 24 CSS pixels.

## Clearance

An online visual similarity review found no matching construction, but that is
not legal clearance. Before commercial registration, run image and word-mark
searches in WIPO and each target market’s national/regional register, then have
trademark counsel review the relevant software and education service classes.
